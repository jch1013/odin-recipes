Cheeseburgers

Description

The cheeseburger is an American classic. This recipe is easy to follow and is highly customizeable depending on what toppings you want. Get started with the list of ingredients below!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (1/3 lb per burger)
  • Hamburger buns
  • Salt and bepper
  • Cheese
  • Any additional toppings

Steps

  1. In a bowl, season ground beef generously with salt and pepper. Mix thoroughly
  2. Form a handful of ground beef into a rough patty shape about 1/2 inch thick
  3. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat
  4. When the pan is warm, add the patty and cook for about 2:30 per side
  5. After the patty is flipped, add a slice of cheese and allow the cheese to melt
  6. While the patty cooks, toast the buns in a toaster
  7. Prepare toppings with extra time
  8. When patty is cooked, place it on top of the bun and add toppings as desired. Enjoy the burger!
