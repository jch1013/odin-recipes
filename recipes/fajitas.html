Chicken Fajitas

Description

This dish is quick and easy to make, and is one of my family's favorite dishes. While this recipe calls for chicken, this can be cooked with any meat. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Fajita Seasoning
  • Onion
  • Bell Peppers
  • Sour Cream
  • Cheese
  • Tortillas
  • Beans (Optional)

Steps

  1. Dice onion while heating a pan with oil on medium heat
  2. Add onion to pan
  3. while onion cooks, slice peppers and add peppers to pan when finished
  4. when the onions and peppers reach the desired consistency, transfer them to a bowl
  5. slice chicken into thin strips and cook on medium heat
  6. After 5 minutes, add seasoning with 1/4 cup water. heat until sauce thickens.
  7. assemble into fajita and enjoy!
