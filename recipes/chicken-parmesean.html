Chicken Parmesean

Description

This recipe is for chicken parmesean, which is a chicken dish with breading and parmesean cheese. In order to make this recipe, you will need a stove and an oven, as well as basic kitchen tools and the necessary ingredients listed below.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Eggs
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a bowl, whisk 2 eggs together
  3. Dip chicken in whisked eggs, and then cover in bread crumbs
  4. cook in skillet on medium heat for 7-10 minutes per side
  5. Coat bottom of cooking dish in tomato sauce and place chicken on top of the sauce
  6. Cover chicken in tomato sauce and parmesean
  7. Bake for 30 minutes at 350
